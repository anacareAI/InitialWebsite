AI Analysis & Hospital Matching

We work with insurance companies to ingest insurer-negotiated rates, hospital price transparency data, and provider quality metrics to estimate the full episode-of-care cost for surgical procedures. When a new surgical claim appears, AnaCare's AI analyzes this data to identify the most cost-efficient, high-quality in-network hospitals and surgeons near the patient.

Analysis: Full episode costs including surgery, facility fees, PT, prescriptions
